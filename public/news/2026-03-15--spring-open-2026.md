# Spring Open Tournament 2026 Announced

We're excited to announce the return of our annual **Spring Open Tournament** on **May 1st, 2026**!

## What's New This Year

This year's tournament features:

- **Expanded Brackets**: Now including beginner, intermediate, and advanced divisions
- **New Categories**: Singles, doubles, and team competitions
- **Increased Prize Pool**: Thanks to our generous sponsors
- **Extended Hours**: Full day event from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM

## Registration Details

- **Opens**: April 1st, 2026
- **Closes**: April 25th, 2026
- **Entry Fee**: CHF 25 per person (singles), CHF 40 per team (doubles)
- **Early Bird Discount**: Register before April 10th and save 20%!

## Location

The tournament will be held at our clubhouse with our newly installed state-of-the-art electronic dartboards and traditional steel dart setups.

**Address**: DC Farmer Clubhouse, [Address to be added]

## How to Register

Contact us via email or visit the clubhouse during regular training hours to sign up. Limited spots available!
