About DC Farmer

Our Mission

DC Farmer is dedicated to promoting the sport of darts in our community through competitive play, skill development, and social engagement. We provide a welcoming environment for players of all skill levels to enjoy both electronic and steel tip darts.

Our History

Founded in [Year], DC Farmer has grown from a small group of darts enthusiasts to a thriving club with competitive teams in both E-Darts and Steel Darts divisions. Our members have achieved numerous successes in regional and district competitions.

Our Values

  • Community: Building lasting friendships through shared passion
  • Excellence: Continuous improvement in skill and sportsmanship
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming players of all backgrounds and abilities
  • Integrity: Fair play and respect in all competitions

Join Us

Interested in becoming a member? We welcome new players year-round. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or just starting out, DC Farmer has a place for you. Contact our committee to learn more about membership options.
